TEN DE TAI DA CHOT

Ứng dụng mô hình hỗn hợp Gaussian (GMM) trong phân cụm khách hàng


GHI CHU CHUNG

Trung tam de tai: bai toan ung dung — phan cum / phan khuc khach hang, cong cu chinh la GMM (hoc phan phoi du lieu, ho tro lay mau / khoi tao cum tuy pipeline cu the).
- Muc tieu: hieu ly thuyet GMM, ap dung len du lieu khach hang (online_retail_II.csv -> RFM hoac tuong duong), danh gia ket qua va y nghia nghiep vu.
- Trong bai viet, tap trung mo ta phuong phap va thuc nghiem cua nhom; tai lieu ben ngoai chi trich dan khi can (khong dat trung tam vao mot bai bao cu the).


TOM TAT PAPER GOC MA MINH DA DOC XONG

1. Bai toan
- Paper giai bai toan phan khuc khach hang bang mo hinh RFM.
- Van de ma tac gia nham den la K-means phu thuoc manh vao tam cum khoi tao, nen ket qua co the khong on dinh.

2. Y tuong cot loi
- Tac gia de xuat framework Gms-Afkmc2.
- "Gms" = lay mau tap con tu du lieu goc bang Gaussian Mixture Model sao cho tap con giu duoc phan bo gan voi du lieu goc.
- "Afkmc2" = dung ASSUMPTION-FREE K-MC2 de tao tam cum khoi tao tot hon K-means va K-means++.
- Sau do dung cac tam cum duoc tao tren tap con de khoi tao va phan cum lai tren tap du lieu goc.

3. Pipeline theo bai bao
- Buoc 1: Chuyen bai toan ve bo thuoc tinh RFM.
- Buoc 2: Dung GMM fit tren du lieu goc.
- Buoc 3: Sinh cac sample tu GMM.
- Buoc 4: Tim cac diem trong du lieu goc gan nhat voi cac sample de tao DSsample.
- Buoc 5: Tren DSsample, dung Afkmc2 de tao cac tam cum khoi tao on dinh.
- Buoc 6: Dung tiep Afkmc2 tren DSraw voi tam khoi tao vua co de lay ket qua phan khuc cuoi cung.

4. Co so toan hoc xuat hien trong paper
- Ham muc tieu cua K-means dua tren tong binh phuong sai so trong cum.
- Elkan K-means duoc nhac den nhu cach tang toc bang bat dang thuc tam giac.
- GMM duoc trinh bay qua phan bo Gaussian cho tung thanh phan va tong hop thanh Gaussian mixture.
- Paper nhac toi toi uu log-likelihood va KL divergence trong mo ta GMM.
- AFK-MC2 duoc mo ta qua phan bo q(x|c1), xich Markov va co che Monte Carlo de chon seed.

5. Dong gop ma tac gia tuyen bo
- Tao duoc seed tot hon cho phan cum.
- Giam so vong lap sau khoi tao.
- Nang chat luong segmentation so voi K-means va K-means++.
- Tim duoc sample rate hop ly, tac gia chon 0.5 la diem can bang tot.


DU LIEU DA CHOT CHO DE TAI (CUA NHOM)

- File: online_retail_II.csv (nam o thu muc goc project GMS_AFKMC2, cung cap voi workspace).
- Quy mo khoang: ~1,067,371 dong (gom 1 dong header), tuong ung ban ghi giao dich muc hang.
- Cac cot trong file:
  - Invoice: ma hoa don
  - StockCode, Description: ma va mo ta hang
  - Quantity: so luong
  - InvoiceDate: thoi gian giao dich
  - Price: don gia
  - Customer ID: dinh danh khach (float trong file, co the NaN)
  - Country: quoc gia
- Huong xu ly de co bang khach hang cho phan cum (RFM), can ghi ro trong bai:
  - Bo cac dong thieu Customer ID (khong gan duoc RFM cho khach vo danh).
  - Xu ly giao dich huy: voi Online Retail II, hoa don bat dau bang chu "C" thuong la huy — can quyet dinh va mo ta ro (loc bo hay xu ly rieng).
  - Monetary: thuong tong (Quantity * Price) theo khach (chi tinh dong hop le).
  - Frequency: thuong so hoa don khac nhau theo khach, hoac so lan mua — chot 1 dinh nghia va thong nhat suot bai.
  - Recency: khoang cach tu "ngay tham chieu" (vi du ngay giao dich moi nhat trong tap) den ngay mua gan nhat cua khach — don vi ngay.
- Sau khi gom theo Customer ID, so khach hang se giam manh so voi so dong giao dich; co the gan voi muc quy mo paper neu tien xu ly tuong tu.


DATASET VA THIET LAP THUC NGHIEM THEO PAPER

1. Dataset 1 (paper) — trung khop voi nguon ta dang dung
- Ten mo ta trong paper: online retail II dataset from Kaggle.
- Quy mo paper ghi: 5878 khach hang, hon 1.04 trieu ban ghi giao dich.
- Tien xu ly paper ghi:
  - Lam sach du lieu.
  - Loai ban ghi trung lap.
  - Dien gia tri thieu.
  - Tao Dataset 1 cuoi cung dua tren RFM.

2. Dataset 2 (paper — tuy chon cho de tai cua minh)
- Paper con dung them mot bo 10,000 khach hang tren Kaggle, khong dinh danh ro.
- De tai "Ung dung GMM trong phan cum khach hang" co the CHI lam tren online_retail_II.csv; neu chi lam 1 bo thi ghi ro trong bai la pham vi thuc nghiem hep hon paper, khong bat buoc tai hien bang so lieu Dataset 2.

3. Thuoc tinh du lieu
- Customer ID
- Recently Purchased Time = Recency
- Frequency of Purchase = Frequency
- Purchase Amount = Monetary

4. So cum
- Tac gia dung elbow method.
- Ket luan k = 4 cho ca hai dataset.

5. Sample rate duoc thu
- 1, 0.8, 0.6, 0.5, 0.4, 0.2, 0.1, 0.05
- Tac gia ket luan 0.5 la su danh doi tot hon giua runtime, so vong lap va chat luong cum.

6. Cac phuong phap duoc so sanh
- Gms-Afkmc2
- K-means++
- K-means

7. Metric danh gia
- SSE
- Silhouette Coefficient (SH)
- Davies-Bouldin Index (DBI)
- Runtime


KET QUA CHINH CUA PAPER

1. Ket qua Dataset 1
- Gms-Afkmc2: SSE = 28.19, SH = 0.92, DBI = 0.42, Runtime = 0.018
- K-means++: SSE = 29.57, SH = 0.90, DBI = 0.44, Runtime = 0.008
- K-means: SSE = 31.25, SH = 0.89, DBI = 0.52, Runtime = 0.051

2. Ket qua Dataset 2
- Gms-Afkmc2: SSE = 241.423, SH = 0.355, DBI = 1.065, Runtime = 0.026
- K-means++: SSE = 245.745, SH = 0.352, DBI = 1.067, Runtime = 0.013
- K-means: SSE = 248.131, SH = 0.347, DBI = 1.071, Runtime = 0.0750

3. Dien giai ket qua
- Theo paper, Gms-Afkmc2 cho ket qua phan cum tot hon nhin theo SSE va SH.
- So vong lap sau khoi tao giam khi sample rate lon hon.
- Doi lai, runtime tao tap con va seed tang len.


NHUNG DIEM PAPER MO TA CHUA DU DE TAI HIEN "Y CHANG"

1. Dataset 2 chua duoc dinh danh ro
- Chi biet do la "public customer segmentation dataset from Kaggle" voi 10,000 khach hang.
- Chua co link chinh xac.
- Chua co ten file, cot du lieu goc, hay cach bien doi sang RFM.

2. Tien xu ly Dataset 1 chua day du
- Chua noi ro cach xu ly giao dich huy/tra hang.
- Chua noi ro moc thoi gian de tinh Recency.
- Chua noi ro cach dien missing value cu the.
- Chua noi ro co loc khach hang khong hop le hay khong.

3. Chuan hoa du lieu chua duoc mo ta ro
- Trong gia ma co bien DSnorm, nhung paper khong noi ro dung Min-Max, Z-score hay cach khac.

4. Vector trong so omega chua duoc mo ta ro
- Paper noi co weight of each component.
- Nhung khong noi ro omega la do nguoi dung dat the nao, co phai [wr, wf, wm] hay cach xac dinh tu du lieu.

5. Cau hinh GMM chua day du
- So thanh phan mixture khong noi ro.
- covariance_type khong noi ro.
- random_state khong noi ro.
- so lan EM hoi tu, tieu chi dung, init params khong noi ro.

6. Cau hinh AFK-MC2 chua day du
- Paper noi dung thu vien kmc2-0.1.
- Nhung khong ghi ro chain length, tham so khoi tao, random seed, cach goi ham cu the.

7. Van de ve metric DBI
- Paper viet o bang mau la "DBI down", tuc la nho hon thi tot hon, day moi la cach hieu chuan.
- Nhung trong phan mo ta van ban co cau "When the index is larger, the clusters are better", cau nay sai voi dinh nghia thong dung cua Davies-Bouldin.
- O bang so sanh cuoi, huong mui ten cua DBI cung bi ghi khong nhat quan.
- Khi tai hien, can thong nhat: DBI nho hon la tot hon.

8. Runtime trong paper rat nho
- Paper bao runtime o muc 0.018, 0.026,...
- Chua ro moi truong phan cung, phien ban thu vien, cach do thoi gian.
- Vi vay runtime co the kho doi chinh xac 100%, ta nen uu tien tai lap xu huong hon la so tuyet doi.

BO CUC TIEN LUAN DE XUAT

1. Tom tat
- Bai toan ung dung phan cum khach hang, vai tro GMM, nguon du lieu (Online Retail II), ket qua va y nghia ngan gon.

2. Gioi thieu
- Bai toan phan khuc khach hang va y nghia thuc tien (marketing, uu tien khach hang).
- Ly do chon mo hinh dua tren GMM trong chuoi xu ly phan cum.
- RFM nhu tap dac trung khach hang; gioi han cua K-means thuan (seed, nhay cam tien xu ly) neu can lam ly do mo rong phuong phap.

3. Co so ly thuyet
- RFM model.
- K-means va ham muc tieu.
- K-means++.
- Elkan K-means.
- Gaussian Mixture Model.
- EM va KL divergence.
- AFK-MC2, xich Markov, Monte Carlo.
- Cac metric SSE, SH, DBI.

4. Phuong phap de xuat / pipeline ung dung
- Mo ta so do tong quat: tu du lieu giao dich -> tien xu ly -> bang khach hang + RFM.
- Vai tro GMM trong bai (vi du: uoc luong phan phoi, lay mau tap con, hoac khoi tao cum — chot dung mot cach va mo ta ro).
- Buoc phan cum cuoi (K-means / bien the da chon) va cac baseline so sanh neu co (K-means++, v.v.).
- Ly do chon tung thanh phan trong pipeline.

5. Thiet ke thuc nghiem
- Mo ta tap du lieu online_retail_II.csv, cac buoc lam sach va quy uoc R/F/M.
- Chon so cum k (elbow hoac tieu chi khac), ly do.
- Cac phuong phap / cau hinh chay thu (GMM, cum, sieu tham so).
- Chi so danh gia: SSE, Silhouette, Davies-Bouldin, thoi gian chay (neu can).

6. Chi tiet cai dat
- Moi truong, thu vien (Python, sklearn, ...).
- Ma hoa / chuan hoa dac trung.
- Tham so GMM, K-means, AFK-MC2 (neu dung) va cach chon.
- Cac lua chon ky thuat va giai thich ngan.

7. Ket qua va phan tich
- Bang so sanh metric giua cac phuong phap.
- Hinh elbow (neu dung).
- Phan bo cum, kich thuoc cum.
- Thong ke RFM (trung binh, trung vi) theo cum — dien giai nghiep vu tung nhom khach.

8. Thao luan
- Nhan xet chat luong cum, do on dinh khi doi seed hoac tien xu ly nhe.
- Anh huong cua chuan hoa va cua cac tham so GMM (neu thu nhieu muc).

9. Ket luan
